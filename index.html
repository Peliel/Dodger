Welcome to Dodger!

You are the purple ball

Move the purple ball by moving your mouse.

Dodge the flying green squares and red circles.

The window narrows as you move your mouse.

Your goal is to stand alone.

When the walls move circles
and squares may enter the wall.

If your ball touches a square, a circle or a wall you die.

You also die if you leave the game window.

You can be invisible for 2.5 seconds
by pressing the left mouse button.

May the luck be with you!
